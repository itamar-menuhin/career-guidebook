default_first_small_step: Find 3 job postings for your target role. List every required skill, then rate your current level (1-5) for each. Identify your top 2 gaps.

When you have a clear target but a skill gap, create a focused learning plan to bridge the gap rather than trying to learn everything.

- Can you clearly name the 2-3 most important skill gaps between you and your target role?
- Are you willing to spend 3-6 months building skills before applying?
- Do you have a track record of self-directed learning?
