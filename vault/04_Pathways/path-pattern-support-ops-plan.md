**Goal:** Multiply the impact of high-leverage researchers or leaders.

**When to suggest:**

- They thrive on **execution and efficiency** ("I love making things run smoothly").
- They are low-ego and service-oriented; happy to be "behind the scenes" if it means the mission succeeds.
- They are good generalists who can handle everything from logistics to HR.

**The Move:** Find the bottleneck. Look for high-impact teams that are slowed down by admin/process friction.
