**Goal:** Move to a new path that builds on what you've learned.

**When to use:**

- You want to change fields but leverage your background.
- You have transferable skills.

**The Move:** Identify paths where your experience gives a head start.

**Examples:** Academic → Policy Analyst; Consultant → Non-profit Ops.

**Small First Step:** Check job boards, spot skill gaps, and draft a plan to close them. **Bonus:** Talk to someone who made a similar move.
