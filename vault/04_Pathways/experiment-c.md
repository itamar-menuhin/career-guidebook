**Goal:** Test if the work suits you by *doing it*.

**When to use:**

- You are drawn to a field but unsure you'll be good at the work.
- You need portfolio items.
- You get energy from making ideas real.

**The Move:** Choose a defined project with a clear output.

**Examples:** Volunteer on a small project; Build a tool filling a gap; Run a pilot with mentorship.

**Small First Step:** Search for organizations offering project frameworks. See what excites you.
