**Goal:** Learn effective/proven domains and mindsets to generate ideas.

**When to use:**

- Unsure where to donate or what problems are important.
- Want a "map" before choosing.
- Want to base career on impact evidence.

**The Move:** Use intro materials/communities to learn about Effective Altruism and global priorities.

**Examples:** *Making a Difference* series; Intro to EA program; 80,000 Hours problem profiles.

**Small First Step:** Choose one entry point (video, program, or article list).
